Privacy Policy for PrimeShot

Last Updated: May 30, 2026

Welcome to PrimeShot by PrimeNova. This Privacy Policy explains how PrimeShot collects, uses, and protects user information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

PrimeShot may request access to the following device features and permissions:

Camera Permission

Used to:

  • Capture photos
  • Record videos
  • Enable camera features inside the app

PrimeShot does not upload your camera content to our servers unless explicitly stated in future updates.

Microphone Permission

Used for:

  • Recording audio during video capture

Audio recordings remain on your device unless you choose to share them.

Storage / Media Permission

Used to:

  • Save captured photos and videos
  • Access media files selected by the user

PrimeShot only accesses files necessary for app functionality.

2. How We Use Information

The permissions and information are used solely to:

  • Provide camera functionality
  • Save and manage captured media
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Enable photography features such as portrait mode, night mode, and face detection

3. Face Detection

PrimeShot may use on-device face detection technology for:

  • Auto focus assistance
  • Portrait effects
  • Camera enhancements

Face detection is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted to external servers.

4. Data Sharing

PrimeShot does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We do not collect sensitive personal information for advertising purposes.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data and app functionality. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

PrimeShot is not directed toward children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Third-Party Services

PrimeShot may use trusted third-party libraries or services such as:

  • Android CameraX
  • Google ML Kit

These services may operate under their own privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Deny permissions through device settings
  • Delete saved media from your device
  • Uninstall the app at any time

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
